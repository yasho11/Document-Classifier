As a new virus took hold, millions of us were told to stay at home and billions of pounds were spent propping up the countrys economy.The Covid inquiry will publish its second set of findings on Thursday, looking in detail at the huge political choices made at the time - including how lockdowns were introduced, the closure of businesses and schools, and bringing in previously unthinkable social restrictions. Did the government serve the people well, or did it fail them? asked the lead counsel at the start of this part of the inquiry in 2023.
