Libraries to host community events over winter27 November 2025ShareSaveAlex McIntyreWest MidlandsShareSavePA MediaThe sessions will be held at libraries across WolverhamptonA series of events are to be held at Wolverhamptons libraries in a bid to bring communities together during the colder months.The Winter Warmth and Wellness sessions will take place at venues across the city between 4 December and 27 January.Councillor Bhupinder Ghakal, of City of Wolverhampton Council, said they would give people a chance to come together and take part in activities that support their wellbeing.The sessions will include hot drinks, mindfulness activities, board games, crafts and puzzles, the local authority said.They will take place on the following datesCentral Library, 4 December, 1500 to 1700 GMTTettenhall Library, 5 December, 1000 to 1145 Bob Jones Community Hub, 9 December, 1800 to 2000Low Hill Library, 12 December, 1500 to 1700 East Park Library, 16 December, 1000 to 1200 Wednesfield Library, 8 January, 1030 to 113 Bob Jones Community Hub, 12 January, 1000 to 1100Finchfield Library, 13 January, 1030 to 1230 Penn Library, 15 January, 1030 to 1230 Bilston Library, 20 January, 1030 to 1230 Whitmore Reans Library, 23 January, 1500 to 1600 Central Library, 27 January, 1030 to 1230 Follow BBC Wolverhampton  Black Country on BBC Sounds, Facebook, X and Instagram.More on this storyDrummer  TV doctor join authors for book festivalFirst stage of city library improvements completedLibraries get 86k to help tackle lonelinessRelated internet linksCity of Wolverhampton CouncilLibrariesWellbeingWolverhampton
